# Code of Conduct

## Introduction

We are committed to creating a friendly and respectful place for learning, teaching, and contributing. All participants in this project and its communications are expected to show respect and courtesy to others.

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces related to this project, including but not limited to the GitHub repository, issue and pull request discussions, and any group or course communication. The project maintainers are expected to assist with enforcement. The course instructional team may be involved in enforcement when the project is used in an academic context.

## Diversity and inclusivity

We are dedicated to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all people, regardless of background or identity. We do not tolerate behaviour that is disrespectful to collaborators or that excludes, intimidates, or causes discomfort to others. We do not tolerate discrimination or harassment based on characteristics that include, but are not limited to: gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, citizenship, nationality, ethnic or social origin, religion or belief, age, education, socio-economic status, technical choices, and experience level.

## Expected behaviour

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of different viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Show courtesy and respect towards other community members.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Written or verbal comments that exclude people on the basis of membership of any specific group.
- Harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory remarks, or personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment or intimidation.
- Publishing others’ private information or communication without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or work.
- Sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, ableist, or otherwise exclusionary jokes or comments.

## Consequences of unacceptable behaviour

Participants who are asked to stop inappropriate behaviour are expected to comply immediately. If someone violates this Code of Conduct, the maintainers may warn the person and/or take further action (e.g. temporary or permanent removal from the repository or discussions). In an academic context, violations may also be reported to the course instructional team.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it by opening an issue in this repository (you can make it private if needed) or by contacting the repository maintainers or the course instructors directly. Reports will be reviewed and handled with appropriate confidentiality.
